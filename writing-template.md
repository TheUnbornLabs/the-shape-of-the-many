# Writing Template — the fixed per-chapter shape

Applied identically to all 100 chapters. Every `chapter.md` follows this skeleton. The seven beats are always present; their length breathes with the concept's depth. Write in prose — the beat headings below are the internal structure, but the finished chapter should read as a continuous essay, not a form. Use the beat headings as `##` sections.

---

```markdown
# NN. <Title>

> <One-sentence epigraph-style statement of the idea — the hook line.>

## The idea
A clear, full statement of the concept in plain language. What is it, in one paragraph a smart non-specialist understands. No jargon before it is earned.

## The origin
The thinker(s), study, or event that established it. Real names, real dates, real sources. Where the idea came from and what problem it was answering. Keep quotations short and attributed.

## The mechanism
Why it works — the causal gears, not just the label. If there is maths (Condorcet, SIR, power laws), state it plainly and show what each term does. The reader should leave able to explain the mechanism to someone else.

## The anchor example
One concrete, vivid case that becomes the reader's memory hook. Specific and occasionally surprising. This is the thing they will remember when they forget the name.

## The flip
How this phenomenon connects to the thesis: what condition tips it toward wisdom or folly, and what its opposite pole is. Cross-reference the paired item in another book by number and name (see the cross-reference map in constants-and-style.md). This beat is where the book's argument lives — never skip or shorten it.

## The human weight
Where it touches real human stakes — leadership, oppression, markets, belief. Surface the frontier thread (stratification / imposed silence / preference falsification) wherever it genuinely applies; leave it out where it would be forced.

## The critique
Its limits, failures to replicate, or contested status. Intellectual honesty, not reverence. Where a classic finding is disputed or WEIRD-sample-bound, say so here. A chapter that only praises its idea has failed this beat.
```

---

## Rules

- **Prose over bullets.** Inside beats, argue in paragraphs. Lists only for genuine enumeration.
- **Beat 5 (The flip) is mandatory and load-bearing.** It is what makes this a book with a thesis rather than a survey.
- **Beat 7 (The critique) is mandatory.** Replication humility is baked into every chapter, not parked in item 100.
- **Every specific claim traces to `references.md`.** Anything specific-but-unconfirmed carries the flag "Further reading to verify before final publication."
- Length ~1,500–3,000 words per chapter, breathing with depth.
- The Shape motif and the thesis restatement should surface naturally at least once per chapter, usually in beat 5.
